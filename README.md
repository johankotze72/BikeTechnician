# BikeTechnician
A tool to enable anyone around the world to be(come) a successful bike mechanic

## HOW
Develop and open-sourced software

### Learning
Teach potential or existing bike mechanics how to fix a bike as easily and entertaining as possible.
- Flashcards (Spaced repetition, Anki, Quizlet) [parts, tools, types...]
- Multi-lingual/opensource (editable subtitles)
- Part Manuals (pdf)
- Voice feedback/motivations (Audible/text readers) {Brilliant, Khan kids}
- Fun animations/ Puzzles,) [systems] {Brilliant, Khan kids}
- Gamification/social (badges, leader boards) {Duolingo}
- Part scanner (image recognition leed to help)
- 3D animated videos
- 3D Virtual workshop

### Business
Own online shop (Shopify)
Buy/sell used bikes
Buy/sell spare parts to other technicians and customers 
Buy in bulk as cooperation
Bookkeeping/Tax returns
Bike Rental/Fleet management
Provide Guarantee
Mechanic ratings
